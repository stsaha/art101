How to Cook Menemem (Turkish Scrambled Eggs)

1. Gather materials:
		- 1 pan
		- 1 spatula
		- 1 chopping board
		- 1 knife
		- 1 bowl (for eggs)
2. Gather ingredients:
		- 1 tablespoon olive oil
		- ½ cup chopped yellow onion (optional)
		- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
		- 2 chopped tomatoes
		- 2 tablespoons tomato paste (optional)
		- 4 beaten eggs
		- Salt and pepper to taste
		- Paprika (optional)
		- Onion powder (optional)
		- Garlic powder (optional)
		- Fresh herbs (optional)
3. Cook recipe:
		- Add oil to a pan and set to medium heat.
		- Add onion and bell pepper. Saute until softened.
		- Add chopped tomatoes and tomato paste. Saute until mixture loosens.
		- Season with salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder, & garlic powder.
		- Pour eggs into the pan. Gently incorporate with tomato mixture.
		- Let eggs cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
		- Top with fresh herbs, such as parsley or oregano.
4. Serve for 2:
		- Prepare with warm bread and enjoy!
			

How to Create a Career-Finding Program

1. List out possible careers incorporated in the program:
		- Teacher
		- Lawyer
		- Doctor
		- Engineer
		- Chef
		- Artist
		- Musician
		- Scientist
		- Entrepreneur
		- Activist
2. Create an array with variables for each career:
		- Use variables of type integer.
		- Array size is 10.
3. Ask the user several questions to understand his/her interests:
		- Prepare list of 10-15 questions.
4. Depending on input, increment career-related variables in the array:
		- If answer shows no releation to career type, do not increment.
		- If answer shows slight relation to career type, increment by +1.
		- If answer shows strong relation to career type, increment by +2.
5. Variable with greatest value in the array at the end is the chosen career:
		- Declare and initalize max variable to first value in array.
		- Use a for-loop to go through entire array.
		- If value of current element is > current max, replace max.
6. Deliver results to the user:
		- Use a print statement.
